Privacy Policy

AfriQ ArtisanSkills is committed to safeguarding personal information and handling data responsibly in line with applicable African data protection laws, including South Africa’s POPIA, and other relevant regulations where we operate.

Home / Privacy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our websites, training experiences, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with AfriQ ArtisanSkills, we may collect contact details (such as name, organisation, email, and phone number), account information, training activity and completion records, support requests, and technical data such as IP address, device information, and browser signals.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide training access, deliver learning pathways, track progress, administer accounts, respond to support requests, improve our content and platform performance, and meet legal and contractual obligations.

4. Legal Basis & African Context

We process personal information based on consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests (such as service improvement and platform security), and legal compliance. Where applicable, we align our practices with South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and similar regional requirements.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

We may share information with service providers who help us operate our platform (such as hosting, analytics, and support tooling). These providers are expected to protect information and use it only for the services they provide to AfriQ ArtisanSkills.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards to protect information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, and alteration. No system is completely secure, but we work to continuously improve our security practices.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including maintaining accounts, delivering training, supporting customers, and meeting legal obligations.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your country and relationship to AfriQ ArtisanSkills, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, objection, or portability. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

9. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to support site functionality, remember preferences, and understand usage patterns. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings and any on-site cookie controls.

10. Contact

Questions or requests about privacy can be sent to privacy@afriqartizanskills.com. If you do not have access to that mailbox, use Contact and we will route your request.
